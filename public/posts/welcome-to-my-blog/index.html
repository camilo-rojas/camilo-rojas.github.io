Welcome to My Blog

I’m excited to launch my new blog! This site is built with Hugo, a fast and flexible static site generator, and styled with a custom theme based on the beautiful Catppuccin Mocha color palette.

What You’ll Find Here

This blog will be a mix of different content types:

  • Long-form posts like this one for detailed thoughts and tutorials
  • Micro posts for quick updates and thoughts
  • Project showcases and development notes

Why Hugo?

I chose Hugo for several reasons:

  1. Speed - Hugo is incredibly fast at building sites
  2. Markdown support - I can write in my favorite markup language
  3. Flexibility - Easy to customize and extend
  4. Static output - Simple to deploy anywhere

The Catppuccin Mocha Theme

The color scheme is based on Catppuccin Mocha, which provides:

  • A cozy dark theme perfect for reading
  • Carefully chosen colors that are easy on the eyes
  • Great contrast ratios for accessibility
  • A cohesive, professional look

I hope you enjoy reading what I have to share!
